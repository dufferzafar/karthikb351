HasGeek

Developer (Dec 2014 - Present)

I joined HasGeek as an intern during my final year of engineering and joined fulltime after I graduated (May 2015).
I started off as the sole Android developer on HasGeek's Talkfunnel app, and then moved on to working on HasGeek's many web products - Hasjob, Talkfunnel, Lastuser, etc. They are all built primarily on Python+Flask+Postgresql, along with HasGeek's custom libraries such as baseframe(UI), coaster(utilities) and many others.

In the latter half of 2015, I served at the editor of droidconIN 2015, HasGeek's android conference. I was responsible for framing the theme, inviting proposals and curating the talks and workshops.
droidconIN 2015 saw 300+ attendees, 20+ talks and 8+ workshops.

#SaveTheInternet

Co-founder (Apr 2015 - Present)

One of India's largest online movements, which saw over 1 million Indians email their telecom regulator in support of Net Neutrality, leading to a major victory in Feb 2016 with the ban on differential pricing.

#SaveTheInternet

Co-founder (Apr 2015 - Present)

I was involved with the campaign from the beginning, helping build the tech that supported the movement.
The entire stack cost us exactly zero. It was built entirely on free tools and services, and managed to handle over 2 million visitors over a span of just 14 days.

I spoke about How we ran #SaveTheInternet for free at Barcamp Bangalore in 2015.
